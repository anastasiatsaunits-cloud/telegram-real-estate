# Moodboard directions — Telegram Real Estate Miniapp

## Direction A — Restrained Luxury
Feeling:
- expensive calm
- black / deep green / warm stone
- understated premium
- editorial typography
- private wealth tone

Best for:
- trust
- premium but not flashy perception
- investment-first audience

Use in product:
- hero
- market cards
- property previews
- typography system

## Direction B — Cinematic Concierge
Feeling:
- atmospheric service
- cinematic backgrounds
- warm blur, glow, layered darkness
- personal concierge mood
- emotional premium entry

Best for:
- wow-effect on first screen
- soft immersive mobile experience
- private access mood

Use in product:
- hero redesign
- transitions
- onboarding feel
- CTA emphasis

## Direction C — Editorial Investment Club
Feeling:
- magazine-grade composition
- strong imagery with restrained overlays
- elegant data presentation
- investment intelligence + luxury polish
- premium decision-support product

Best for:
- combining beauty and analytical trust
- premium cards and detail screens
- market credibility

Use in product:
- market cards
- detail page
- ROI / metrics blocks
- recommendation screens

## Recommended blend
Primary direction:
- Direction A (Restrained Luxury)

Amplifier:
- Direction B for hero and motion atmosphere

Structural support:
- Direction C for cards, detail pages, and investment framing

## Working decision
The miniapp should not become flashy luxury.
It should be:
- restrained
- expensive
- calm
- private
- cinematic in the first impression
- editorial in structure
